Creme Brulee

Description

A decadent desert recipe for dinners at home, creamy with crunchy caramelized top.

Ingredients

for 4 servings

  • 2 cups light cream or half-and-half
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 5 egg yolks
  • 1/3 cup white sugar, more for topping.

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 150 degree Celcius.
  2. Heat cream in a saucepan until hot. Let it sit some minutes. Add vanilla extract.
  3. In a mixing bowl, add yolk and sugar. Whisk by hand until light.
  4. Stir 1/4 cup cream into the mixture until well-mixed. Then pour the mixture into the cream. Stir until combined.
  5. Pour the mixture into four six-ounce ramekins. Load onto a baking dish.
  6. Fill the baking dish until water reaches halfway up the side.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes, or until centers are barely set. Cool.
  8. To serve. Top each ramekin with a teaspoon of sugar. Broil 2-3 inches from the heat source for five minutes.
  9. Serve within two hours.
